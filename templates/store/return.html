Returns & Refunds Policy

  1. Home
  2. Return

At Nimayah Jewellery, we strive to ensure your utmost satisfaction with every purchase. If you are not entirely satisfied with your order, we offer hassle-free returns and refunds within a specified timeframe. Please read the following guidelines carefully:

Eligibility for Returns and Refunds:
  • Items must be returned within 7 days of the purchase date.
  • The item must be in its original condition, unworn, undamaged, and with all original tags and packaging intact.
Non-Eligible Items for Returns and Refunds:
  • Customized or personalized items.
  • Earrings (for hygiene reasons).
  • Items that show signs of wear and tear or damage due to mishandling.
Return Process:
  • To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at 9876543210 within 7 days of receiving your order.
  • Provide your order number and a brief explanation of the reason for the return.
  • Once your return request is approved, we will provide you with a return authorization and instructions on how to return the item.
Refund Process:
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Please allow up to 10 days for the refund to be processed after we receive the returned item.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to a mistake on our part.
Exchanges:
  • We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item, please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.
Damaged or Defective Items:
  • In the rare event that you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately upon receiving the order.
  • We will arrange for a replacement or issue a full refund, including shipping costs, upon return of the damaged or defective item.
Return Shipping:
  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a mistake on our part.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service for returns, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.
International Returns:
  • International customers are subject to the same return policy; however, return shipping costs and customs fees are the responsibility of the customer.
Final Sale Items:
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for returns or refunds unless they arrive damaged or defective.
Contact Us:
  • If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Returns & Refunds Policy, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team at [contact email/phone number].

Please note that our Returns & Refunds Policy is subject to change without prior notice. We reserve the right to refuse any returns that do not comply with the above guidelines. Thank you for choosing Nimayah Jewellery. We appreciate your business!
